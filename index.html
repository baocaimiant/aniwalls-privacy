AniWalls Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

AniWalls does not collect any personally identifiable information. No account or personal details are required.

2. Advertising

AniWalls uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Wallpaper Images

Wallpapers are sourced from Wallhaven (wallhaven.cc). AniWalls does not host or own any images. All images are subject to their respective source licenses.

4. Saved Favorites

Your favorite wallpapers are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

5. Device Permissions

Storage / Photo Library: Required only to save wallpapers to your gallery, accessed only when you press Save.
Notifications: Used only for a daily wallpaper reminder. You can disable this in device settings.
Set Wallpaper: Allows the app to apply a wallpaper to your home or lock screen.

6. Third-Party Services

AniWalls integrates with Wallhaven API (wallpaper content) and Google AdMob (advertising). These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

AniWalls is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
